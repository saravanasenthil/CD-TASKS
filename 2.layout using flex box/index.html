Let's Learn

eLearning, also known as electronic learning or online learing, is the delivery of learning and training through digital resources.Different studies have found that participants learn five times more material in online learning courses using multimedia content than in traditional face to face courses. Because online courses give students full control over their own learning, students are able to work at their own speed E-learning, which delivers learning content through digital platforms such as websites, apps, or software, can be hugely beneficial to an e-commerce business. By reducing the need for physical classrooms, travel, and materials, e-learning can save time and money.
